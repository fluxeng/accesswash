Troubleshooting

  • Console Backend: Check the Django console output for emails
  • SMTP Issues: Verify your email credentials and security settings
  • Gmail: Use App Passwords instead of your regular password
  • Firewall: Ensure ports 587 (TLS) or 465 (SSL) are open
  • Template Issues: Check Django logs for template rendering errors

Gmail Setup Instructions

  1. Enable 2-Factor Authentication on your Gmail account
  2. Go to Google Account → Security → App passwords
  3. Generate an app password for "Mail"
  4. Use this app password in your EMAIL_HOST_PASSWORD setting
